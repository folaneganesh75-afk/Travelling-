Welcome

Maharashtra is famous for its rich history and majestic forts built during the Maratha Empire. These forts are popular tourist destinations for trekking, history lovers, and nature enthusiasts.

Raigad Fort

Raigad Fort was the capital of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's kingdom. Located in Raigad district, it offers breathtaking views and historical importance.

  • Location: Raigad District
  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • Famous for: Shivaji Maharaj's coronation

Sinhagad Fort

Sinhagad Fort is known for the historic battle fought by Tanaji Malusare. It is a popular trekking destination near Pune.

  • Location: Near Pune
  • Best time to visit: Monsoon & Winter
  • Famous for: Battle of Sinhagad

Pratapgad Fort

Pratapgad Fort is famous for the battle between Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan. It is located near Mahabaleshwar.

  • Location: Satara District
  • Best time to visit: October to February
  • Famous for: Battle of Pratapgad
